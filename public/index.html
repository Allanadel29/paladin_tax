About Us

We are here to help your business thrive

Paladin is a Zambian professional tax and advisory firm specializing in tax compliance, transfer pricing, and regulatory advisory services for businesses in Zambia.

We provide expert services in Transfer Pricing documentation and policy formulation, Transfer Pricing audit support and engagement with the Zambia Revenue Authority (ZRA), Comprehensive Transfer Pricing Health Checks, Tax advisory, Corporate Income Tax, VAT compliance, payroll management, Withholding Tax filings, and statutory compliance services including NAPSA and NHIMA.

Our team comprises experienced tax practitioners with strong local and international tax expertise, combining in-depth knowledge of Zambian tax legislation with global best practices. We work closely with our clients to ensure regulatory compliance, manage tax risk, and support informed business and investment decisions.

At Paladin, we are committed to professionalism, integrity, and delivering practical solutions in an evolving tax and regulatory environment.
